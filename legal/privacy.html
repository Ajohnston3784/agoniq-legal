Privacy Policy

AgonIQ  ·  Last updated April 2026

Overview

AgonIQ is built with your privacy as a first principle. Your personal fitness data stays on your device by default. We do not sell, share, or monetise your data.

Data We Collect

AgonIQ stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Workout history — completed sessions, sets, reps, weights, and run distances
  • Training plans — scheduled sessions generated by the plan builder
  • Personal records — best lifts per exercise
  • Body metrics — optional bodyweight and body-fat log entries
  • Coach chat history — messages between you and the AI coach
  • App settings — your display name, unit preferences, and coach mode

All of this data is stored in a SQLite database on your device. It is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party storage service.

AI Coach & Third-Party Services

When you send a message to the AI coach, the following information is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a response:

  • Your message text
  • Recent training context — your last lift weight and reps for the current session's first exercise, most recent personal record, days since last session, weekly completion count, and current streak
  • Conversation history from the current chat session

This data is processed by OpenAI in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not send your full workout logs, body metrics, or training plan details to OpenAI unless you explicitly paste them into the chat.

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)
Subscription purchases are processed via RevenueCat, a third-party payment infrastructure provider. RevenueCat receives your anonymised app user ID and purchase receipt data to validate and manage your subscription. No personal fitness data is shared with RevenueCat. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

No other third-party data processing services are used.

Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard secure storage sandbox. No account, login, or cloud sync is required to use the app.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed by the operating system.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can delete any or all of your data at any time from Profile → Delete Data. The following categories can be deleted independently:

  • Workout history
  • Training plan
  • Personal records
  • Body metrics
  • Chat history

A full reset option is also available that removes all data and resets your profile. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

AgonIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us so we can assist.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app store listing or the project's GitHub repository.
